Burnt Cheesecake


BASE INGREDIENTS
  • 4 cups of Marie biscuits crushed
  • 1 cup of roughly chopped walnut
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2cup of icing sugar

    • Step 1:mix all ingredients and mix well.
    Step 2:press the base mixture into a 22cm baking pan.
    Step 3:bake for 15 minutes at an oven temperature of 175 degrees Celsius
    Step 4: cool down it.

    THE ESSENCE INGREDIENTS
  • 3 tablespoons of custard flour
  • 3 tablespoons of water
  • 455g of cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups of castor sugar
  • a pinch of salts
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla essence
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 2 cups of sour cream
  • 6 eggs(egg whites and egg yolks separated)
  • 1 teaspoon of lemon zest
  • slices of lemon for garnishing

    • Step 1:dissolve custard flour with water
    Step 2:Beat cream cheese and sugar until fluffy
    Step 3:add salt, vanilla essence and lemon juice.
    Step 4:Beat until smooth
    Step 5:add sour cream, egg yolk and lemon zest
    Step 6: in a separate bowl, beat the egg whites until thick and gently whisk into the cream cheese mixture
    Step 7:pour into a baking pan containing the base already made earlier.
    Step 8:bake at 170 degrees Celsius for 1 hour
    Step 9: let the cheesecake cool in the oven by opening the oven door for at least 1 hour.
    Step 10:garnish with lemon slices
