Walnut Carrot Cake with Orange Cream


INGREDIENTS
  • 4 eggs
  • 3/4 cups of corn oil
  • 390 g custor sugar
  • 255 g bread flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salts
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon bicarbonat of soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 455 g shredded carrots
  • 75 g chopped walnut

    • Step 1:using an electric mixer, whisk the eggs until light and frothy.
    Step 2:add oil slowly.
    Step 3:reduce speed and add sugar.
    Step 4: continue the rotation by increasing the speed for another 3-4 minutes until the mixer is fluffy.
    Step 5: sift flour, salt, cinnamon bark powder, bicarbonate of soda, and baking powder.
    Step 6:add carrots and walnuts.
    Step 7:fold in the batter until smooth.
    Step 8:pour into a greased 25cm cake mold.
    Step 9:bake at 190 degrees Celsius for 50 minutes.

    ORANGE CREAM CHEESE FROSTING INGREDIENTS
  • 220g cheese cream
  • 60g unsalted butter smoothen
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla essence
  • 1 1/2 cups of castor sugar
  • 1 orange-peel skin
  • 1 orange for the juice heated until recieve 1 tablespoon of it
  • 3 tablespoons of fruit juice
  • carrots grater for garnishing

    • Step 1:set the electric mixer on low speed and turn the cream cheese.
    Step 2: slowly add the butter.
    Step 3:add vanilla essence, powdered sugar, grated orange peel, orange juice and fruit juice. do not over -rotate, rotate until smooth and even.
    Step 4:apply frosting on the cake and sprinkle grated carrots on top of the cake.
