Almond Crunch Biscuits


ALMOND CRUCH BISCUITS INGREDIENTS
  • 170g butter without sugar
  • 200g icing sugar
  • 4 eggs yolk
  • 1/2cup of icing sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla essence
  • 285g all-purpose flour
  • 170g cornflour
  • 140g almond powder
  • 1/2 cup of almond slivered for garnishing
  • small amount of egg yolk to smear later

    • Step 1:Beat butter and icing sugar until creamy.
    Step 2:add egg yolk, vanilla essence and almond essence. mix well.
    Step 3:add both types of flour and almond powder.
    Step 4: mix the dough into a dough and store in the refrigerator for 1 hour before kneading it.
    Step 5: apply with a biscuit mold according to the desired shape.
    Step 6:place silvered almonds on top.
    Step 7:smear with egg yolk and bake for 12-15 minutes at 175 degrees Celsius until golden yellow.
